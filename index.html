RideLink
Overview

The data on this site comes from the New York Taxi Fare and Mileage Information Database. We hope to use this data to help drivers and passengers plan their journeys more wisely, and in doing so, improve resource efficiency and reduce unnecessary carbon emissions. We hope that through this website, we can do our part to reduce carbon emissions and the greenhouse effect on this blue planet that we all depend on. We sincerely hope that our small gesture can influence more people to protect our only precious home.
